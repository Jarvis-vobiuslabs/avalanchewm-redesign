About Avalanche

Built on Honesty. Driven by Community.

Avalanche Waste Management has established itself as an innovative leader in Calgary's bin rental, waste, and recycling industry. We provide Calgary and surrounding areas with residential and commercial bin rentals, portable toilets, and temporary fencing.

Our bins are manufactured right here in Southern Alberta — supporting local businesses while delivering best-in-class equipment. A large majority of the junk and waste we haul is recycled and repurposed, diverting materials from landfills as a core part of our business model.

We specialize in making waste management one less problem on your plate. Whether you're a busy site supervisor or a homeowner tackling a renovation, we show up on time, charge a fair price, and take care of the rest.
